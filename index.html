📊 YouTube History Tracker

📝 How to get Data (100% Safe Method)

  1. Go to Google Takeout
  2. Click "Deselect all", check only YouTube and YouTube Music
  3. Click the All YouTube data included button.
  4. Important! Uncheck everything EXCEPT "History" and "Comments".
  5. Click "Multiple formats" and change History format to JSON.
  6. Click "Next step" -> "Export". You will get watch-history.json and a comments file (.csv). Upload both here.
🕵️‍♂️ Not going to steal your data (No money for server anyway). 100% runs in your browser.
